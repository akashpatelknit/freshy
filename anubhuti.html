Anubhuti

Last time Anubhuti held on 2k19 from 22nd February to 25th February 2019 in KNIT, Sultanpur.
Anubhuti hosts various events from various genres ranging from Fine Arts and Photography, Dance and Music to Theatre and Literary events.
To end each day in a memorable way, we have a special night which includes Star Nights, Kavi Sammelan, EDM Night and a Band Night. Stars like Gajendra Verma, Shirley Setia, Munawwar Rana, Vineet Chauhan, Padmini Sharma, DJ Sartek etc have glorified our Anubhuti.
